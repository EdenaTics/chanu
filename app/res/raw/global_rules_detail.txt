<p>1. Do not upload, post, discuss, request, or link to, anything that violates local or United States law. This will be severely punished and strictly enforced.</p>
<p>2. If you are under the age of 18, or it is illegal for you to view the materials contained on this website, discontinue browsing immediately.</p>
<p>3. Do not post the following outside of /b/: Trolls, flames, racism, off-topic replies, uncalled for catchphrases, macro image replies, indecipherable text (example: "lol u tk him 2da bar|?"), anthropomorphic ("furry"), grotesque ("guro"), post number GETs ("dubs"), or loli/shota pornography.</p>
<p>4. The posting of personal information or calls to invasion is prohibited.</p>
<p>5. All boards that default to the Yotsuba B or Burichan (blue) theme are to be considered "work safe." Violators may be temporarily banned and their posts removed. Note: Spoilered pornography or other not safe for work content is NOT allowed.</p>
<p>6. The quality of posts is extremely important to this community. Contributors are encouraged to provide high-quality images and informative comments.</p>
<p>7. Submitting false reports or otherwise abusing the report system will result in a ban of indeterminate length. Replying to a thread stating that you've reported it or another post is also disallowed&mdash;please do not announce your reports.</p>
<p>8. Complaining about 4chan (its policies, moderation, etc.) on the imageboards can result in post deletion and banishment. <p>9. Ban evasion will result in permanent bans&mdash;no exceptions. Instead, wait and <a href="/faq#banappeal">appeal</a> it!</p>
<p>10. No spamming or flooding of any kind.</p>
<p>11. Advertising (all forms) is also not welcome&mdash;this includes any type of referral linking, "offers", stream threads, etc.</p>
<p>12. Impersonating the 4chan administrator, moderators, or janitors is strictly forbidden.</p>
<p>13. Do not use avatars or attach signatures to your posts.</p>
<p>14. The use of scrapers, bots, or other automated posting or downloading scripts is prohibited. Users may not post from proxies or Tor exit nodes.</p>

<p>16. All requests belong on /<a href="//boards.4chan.org/r/" title="Request">r</a>/ unless otherwise noted.</p>
