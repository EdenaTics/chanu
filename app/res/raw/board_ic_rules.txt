<h3>/ic/ - Artwork/Critique</h3>

<p>1. User-drawn works are to be submitted for critiquing. Do not submit work that you have not personally drawn or contributed to.</p>
<p>2. Only constructive criticism will be accepted. Rude or offensive comments will result in a ban.</p>
<p>3. Requests for free work of any sort are forbidden.</p>
<p>4. Discussion of drawing tablets belongs in /g/.</p>
