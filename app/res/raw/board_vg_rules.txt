<h3>/vg/ - Video Game Generals</h3>

<p>1. This board is for the posting of video game "general" threads, which are long-term, recurring threads about a specific topic.</p>
<p>2. Western visual novels should be posted here, and Japanese visual novels on <a href="http://boards.4chan.org/jp/">http://boards.4chan.org/jp/</a>. Translated visual novels are allowed on either board.</p>
