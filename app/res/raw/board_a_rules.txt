<h3>/a/ - Anime &amp; Manga</h3>
<p>1. All images and resulting discussion should pertain to anime or manga. For long-term series discussion, please refer to the <a href="http://dis.4chan.org/anime/">http://dis.4chan.org/anime/</a> board.</p>
<p>2. The use of spoiler tags is highly recommended. To spoilerize text, enclose it like so: [spoiler]spoiled text[/spoiler]. In addition, images may be spoilerized by checking the "[Spoiler Image?]" box on the submission form.</p>
<p>3. Purposeful spoiling of a series may result in post deletion and temporary ban.</p>
<p>4. The discussion of "live action" television shows is permitted so long as they are distinctly rooted in, or based off of an anime or manga series.</p>
<p>5. Japanese visual novels should be posted in <a href="http://boards.4chan.org/jp/">http://boards.4chan.org/jp/</a> , and Western on <a href="http://boards.4chan.org/vg/">http://boards.4chan.org/vg/</a> .</p>
