<h3>/p/ - Photography</h3>

<p>1. Only upload images that you, the photographer, have taken.</p>
<p>2. Post only photos that show at least trace amounts of thoughtful composition. Do not upload random snapshots.</p>
<p>3. Even though EXIF data is made available when encoded, please post as much relevant technical information as possible, such as: camera, kit, lens, etc.</p>
<p>4. Include a short description with your photograph, such as when and where it was photographed and under what circumstances.</p>
<p>5. As with the <em>art</em> critique board, only constructive criticism will be tolerated.</p>
