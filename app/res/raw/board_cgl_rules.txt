<h3>/cgl/ - Cosplay &amp; EGL</h3>

<p>1. Pictures depicting those in costume play ("cosplay") and elegant gothic lolita ("EGL") dress are welcome.</p>
<p>2. Don't bring community drama into this board. Singling out individual cosplayers and trolling them will not be tolerated.</p>
<p>3. J-fashion is allowed. Models can be of any ethnicity, but the fashion should be of Japanese origin.</p>
<p>4. "What Are You Wearing Today" threads should be cosplay/EGL/J-fashion focused. General fashion threads should be posted to /fa/.</p>
<p>5. Health threads belong on /adv/ or /fit/.</p>
